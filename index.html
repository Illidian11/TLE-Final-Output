    Live, Love, Dream

    What is Self-Love?

    Self-love is exhibited by accepting oneself completely, treating oneself with care and respect, and promoting one's own growth and well-being. Self-love is concerned with how you treat yourself as well as your ideas and feelings about yourself. Think about what you'd do for yourself, how you'd talk to yourself, and how you'd feel about yourself if you loved and cared for yourself. A deep devotion to one's own pleasure and well-being is defined as self-love. Self-love is focusing on one's own needs rather than those of others. Accepting less than what you deserve demonstrates self-love.


    Why have Self-Love?

    You are more likely to be self-critical and fall to people-pleasing and perfectionism if you do not love yourself. Being assertive, developing positive connections with others, and exercising self-care all begin with self-love.


    We accept the love that we think we deserve. - ChBosky

    Aspects Of Self-Love

    Being self-aware is being aware of your mental processes, such as your thoughts, how they affect your emotions, and how emotions drive your actions. Are you aware of the thoughts that enrage you and drive you to act rashly? What makes you so happy about it? Self-awareness is the cornerstone to emotional intelligence. If you can't go away or escape the situation, self-awareness helps you to redirect the energy you're putting into those feelings. One method for increasing self-awareness is to keep a record of your thoughts, emotions, and activities.

    Our beliefs about ourselves are referred to as our self-worth, and we frequently struggle to believe in ourselves. All of your positive qualities add to your feeling of self-worth. Set aside a day to think on the things you have done well or on which others have commended you if you are trying to uncover your self-worth. You can be a pushover because you don't recognize your own worth. There is never a day when you are unworthy. Recognize and be aware of this. Your skills, talents, and acts of goodwill to others are all manifestations of your self-worth. Self-esteem develops from self-worth. A high sense of self-worth is followed by a strong sense of self-esteem. Self-worth is the belief that we are valuable regardless of what we have done or the characteristics we possess. Our talents and successes are more closely tied to our self-esteem. As you gain a sense of self-worth, self-esteem will flow more easily. If you want to feel better about yourself, work on your self-worth. Remind yourself every day that you do not need to justify your existence. Self-care encompasses all of the actions we engage in to keep ourselves well, such as bathing, eating nutritious foods, staying hydrated, and doing things we love. When compared to the other components of self-love, self-care is the simplest to practice.




    Life is a Fight

    In life, Self-Love is no recommendation. It is a requirement that you need in your journey in your very own life. Let's say "Mistakes, are your lessons, studies, are your training, knowledge is your weapon, self-love, is your armor and shield, challenges in life are your enemies, the world is your battlefield, and dreams and goals are your trophies." Meaning, when moving forward in life, you needed these things to succesfully control your own life.
